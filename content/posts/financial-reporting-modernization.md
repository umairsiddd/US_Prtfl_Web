## Project Overview

Led the strategic migration and modernization of 100+ enterprise financial reports from legacy Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 to Dynamics 365 Finance, for clients across the United States, Australia, New Zealand, and Saudi Arabia.

## Challenge

The client's legacy AX 2012 reporting infrastructure was outdated, causing delays in period-end financial closing, reduced data accuracy, and limited real-time analytics capabilities for executive decision-making.

## Solution

- Migrated **100+ SSRS and Docentric reports** from AX 2012 to D365 Finance
- Redesigned report templates to align with modern financial reporting standards
- Optimized inventory visibility, sales analytics, and invoicing processes
- Authored comprehensive **Functional Design Documents (FDDs)** and **Technical Design Documents (TDDs)** for each migration phase
- Collaborated with finance stakeholders across multiple time zones to validate report accuracy

## Technologies Used

`Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance` `Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012` `SSRS` `Docentric` `X++` `SQL` `Azure DevOps`

## Business Impact

- Improved **financial data accuracy** and reporting agility across the enterprise
- Significantly **reduced time required** for period-end closing cycles
- Enhanced decision-support analysis for executive and finance leadership teams
- Delivered across international regulatory environments (US, AU, NZ, KSA)
