## Project Overview

Developed a proprietary, dynamic pricing calculator for Kaizen Copilot (Retrocausal.ai), enabling prospects to receive instant, accurate cost estimates — directly shortening the sales cycle and improving the customer acquisition process.

## Challenge

Prospects visiting the Kaizen Copilot product page had no mechanism to self-estimate service costs, forcing them to schedule discovery calls before understanding pricing. This created unnecessary friction in the early sales funnel.

## Solution

- Designed and developed a **custom dynamic pricing calculator** integrated directly into the product landing page
- Built complex business logic into the calculator to generate accurate, real-time cost estimates based on user inputs
- Integrated the tool with **HubSpot CRM** to capture prospect data automatically upon interaction
- Ensured the calculator was fully **responsive and performance-optimized** across all devices
- Collaborated with sales leadership to align pricing logic with commercial strategy

## Technologies Used

`JavaScript` `HTML` `CSS` `HubSpot CRM` `WordPress` `Google Analytics 4` `Google Tag Manager`

## Business Impact

- **Significantly improved customer acquisition** by delivering immediate value at the top of the funnel
- **Shortened the sales cycle** by removing the need for early-stage discovery calls on pricing
- Increased prospect **engagement time** on the product page
- Provided the sales team with **pre-qualified, intent-rich leads** from CRM capture data
