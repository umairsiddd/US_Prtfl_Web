## Project Overview

Architected and implemented an end-to-end lead management and sales automation system for Retrocausal.ai, integrating HubSpot CRM with custom web landing pages, Google Analytics 4, and automated nurturing workflows.

## Challenge

The sales and marketing team lacked a unified system to track leads, measure campaign performance, and automate customer engagement. This created bottlenecks in the sales pipeline and limited visibility into conversion performance.

## Solution

- **Managed and optimized HubSpot CRM** — lead capture workflows, contact management, automation, pipeline organization, and reporting
- Designed and developed **high-converting landing pages and research portals** with integrated HubSpot forms
- Implemented **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** and **Google Tag Manager (GTM)** with custom event tracking
- Integrated HubSpot CRM with GA4 for end-to-end lead generation tracking
- Improved website SEO using **Yoast SEO**, XML sitemaps, canonical URLs, and redirection strategies
- Managed **third-party API integrations** ensuring seamless communication between platforms

## Technologies Used

`HubSpot CRM` `Google Analytics 4` `Google Tag Manager` `WordPress` `HTML` `CSS` `JavaScript` `Yoast SEO`

## Business Impact

- Streamlined the **sales pipeline** with automated lead qualification workflows
- Provided the sales team with a **data-backed customer journey view** from first touch to conversion
- Increased **search engine visibility** and organic traffic through structured SEO improvements
- Enabled marketing leadership to measure campaign ROI in real time
